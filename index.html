Better With Time

Pensey's Club is a place where traditions and modernity meet.

Pensey's is dedicated to preserving and cherishing best golf traditions along with adapting the newest gear and technical advancement.

When keeping the old spirit, we also strive to cultivate a modern approach to the game, which would make it more attractive to young players. Judging that we already have 100 regular premium club members and 400 kids in the Academy, we'd say it's going well.

/ Amenities /

Space for Communication

Golf is a perfect game for meeting new people, networking, and doing business in informal atmosphere.

For these purposes, we have 5 open-air spaces and chill-out zones, and a dining room that is available for all members and visitors. Our club remembers the conversations of legendary players that had place almost 100 years ago. It soaked the essential atmosphere of the golf passion and inspires game lovers today to possibly become legendary one day as well.

The atmosphere, however, is not the only thing we are proud of. First and foremost, our club is also a place where our guests enjoy drinks and meals and discuss the game.
